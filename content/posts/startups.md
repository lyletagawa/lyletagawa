##

99% of people are not cut out to join a startup.


At a startup, things break. Plans change.

You build the plane while flying it, and the destination shifts mid-flight.

That’s not a red flag.
That is the job.

The best startup hires:
- Spot problems and fix them without being asked
- Care more about outcomes than org charts
- Stay calm when everything feels like it’s on fire

If you’re hiring at the early stage, don’t ask:
”Can they do the job?”

Ask:
”Can they define the job?”
